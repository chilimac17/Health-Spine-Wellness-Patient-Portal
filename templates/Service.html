Neuropathy

Do you have signs and symptoms of neuropathy in Belleville? Neuropathy is also known as peripheral neuropathy because of its effect on the body's sensory and motor nerves. It is very common for patients to experience the first symptoms in their extremities (hands, arms, legs and feet). When this occurs it is referred to as Sensorimotor Polyneuropathy.

Are you looking for help with physiotherapy in Belleville? The team at Health & Spine Wellness Center is here to help.
